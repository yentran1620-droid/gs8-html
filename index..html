GRAMMAR REVIEW

Past Continuous, Past Simple, Adverbs of Time & Wh-Questions

(Ôn tập: Quá khứ tiếp diễn, Quá khứ đơn, Trạng từ chỉ thời gian & Câu hỏi Wh-)

1

Multiple Choice (Trắc nghiệm)

Choose the best answer to complete each sentence.

1. When I __________ home, my mom was cooking dinner.

2. She went to the park __________ she finished her homework.

3. __________ you playing football at 5 pm yesterday?

4. I will call you as soon as I __________ at the airport.

5. __________ do you go to the gym? - Twice a week.

Write sentences using Past Continuous and Past Simple

Write complete sentences using the suggested words. Pay attention to the verb tenses.

1. They / do / homework / 8 p.m. last night

2. She / read / a book / this time yesterday

3. What / you / do / at 9 a.m. yesterday?

4. He / drive / to work / when / it / rain

5. While / they / play / football, / it / start / to rain

6. What / she / do / from 6 to 8 yesterday evening?

7. 7 o’clock / yesterday morning, / I / have / breakfast

8. they / watch / TV / 10 p.m. last night?

3

Combine sentences using adverbs of time

Combine the sentences using: when, while, before, after, as soon as, until.

1. I was walking in the park. I saw a strange dog. (when)

2. She was cooking dinner. The lights went out. (while)

3. We finished the test. We left the classroom. (after)

4. I will tell you the truth. I see you tomorrow. (when)

5. He brushed his teeth. He went to bed. (before)

6. I will call you. I arrive home. (as soon as)

7. Stay here. I come back. (until)

8. It gets dark. We usually go home. (when)

4

Make questions for the underlined parts

Write the question for the word in brackets.

1. She is reading a book. (What)

2. They went to school yesterday. (When)

3. He is playing football in the park. (Where)

4. She studies English every day. (How often)

5. They were watching TV last night. (What)

6. We can save electricity by turning off the lights. (How)

7. We should use reusable bags to protect the environment. (Why)

8. We should choose the blue T-shirt. (Which)

9. They are planting trees in the park now. (Where)

10. She goes to school by bike every day. (How)
